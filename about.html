About KOREX FC

Founded: 2008

Location: TAU

Motto: "Never Give Up"

Colors: Purple and White

KOREX Football Club was founded in 2008 in the heart of TAU, driven by a collective vision to develop raw football talent and foster unity through sport. What began as a small neighborhood team has grown into a competitive club recognized for its discipline, resilience, and consistent performance. With purple and white as its symbolic colors, KOREX FC reflects ambition, pride, and loyalty both on and off the pitch.

Over the years, the club has nurtured countless young players who have gone on to represent higher leagues and national tournaments. KOREX FC places a strong emphasis on teamwork, strategy, and community impact. The club’s motto, "Never Give Up", is more than a slogan—it's a daily mindset instilled in every player, coach, and supporter who walks through the gates.

Beyond football, KOREX FC is a family. We aim to inspire the next generation not only to succeed in sports but to excel in life. From local outreach programs to youth training camps, the club continues to build bridges and strengthen bonds within the TAU community. With every match, we play not just for glory, but for the pride of our people and the future of the game.
